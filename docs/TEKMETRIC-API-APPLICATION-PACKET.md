## 1. One-line positioning

TekBox is a **knowledge platform** that sits **on top of** shop management systems to give service advisors provenance-backed vehicle intelligence — it does **not** replace Tekmetric.

## 3. Product vision (credible, not speculative)

Blind Teknologis builds TekBox so shops can:

- Recover missed service opportunities with clearer vehicle and maintenance context
- Reduce advisor time spent assembling tribal knowledge and paperwork
- Communicate recommendations with evidence — and **UNKNOWN** when a fact is unverified
- Keep Tekmetric (or another SMS) as the system of record

## 5. Technical seriousness (architecture commitments)

- **Vendor-neutral core** — Tekmetric-specific mapping lives in adapters only  
- **Raw source preservation** — normalized objects retain source payload context  
- **Provenance mandatory** — capability / maintenance / knowledge facts cite sources  
- **Fail closed** — unmatched or unverified data does not silently substitute another vehicle  
- **UNKNOWN is first-class** — honest uncertainty over false certainty
